About Me

Hello!

As a cloud-native security professional with over 12 years of experience, I specialize in designing and implementing robust security architectures across AWS and Azure. My expertise lies in translating business risk into actionable, resilient controls, a skill honed through leading global projects that protect thousands of endpoints and ensure critical regulatory compliance, including PCI DSS. My approach combines strategic architecture with hands-on technical proficiency in a wide range of security technologies (SASE, EDR/XDR, DLP, IDS/IPS, and more). I am adept at integrating security into CI/CD pipelines to accelerate secure development and have a proven track record of architecting secure, scalable solutions for complex, multi-cloud environments. A collaborative leader, I excel at mentoring high-performing teams and engaging with stakeholders to strengthen cybersecurity defenses and drive business continuity.

In my free time, I enjoy tinkering with computers and swimming.
